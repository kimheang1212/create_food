Tool You'll Need to Make Our Best Baked Salmon Recipe

  • Baking Dish: Use a baking dish large enough to hold the salmon fillets comfortably without overcrowding.
  • Aluminum Foil or Parchment Paper: Either of these will help to wrap the salmon fillets while baking, ensuring they cook evenly and stay moist.
  • Knife: A sharp knife will be necessary for cutting the salmon fillets and any additional ingredients, such as herbs or lemon slices.
  • Cutting Board: Use a clean cutting board to prepare the salmon and any accompanying ingredients.
  • Measuring Spoons: For accurately measuring out ingredients like spices and seasonings.
  • Oven: A conventional oven for baking the salmon to perfection.
  • Optional: Basting Brush or Spoon: If you plan to baste the salmon with any sauces or marinades while baking, a basting brush or spoon will come in handy.

    • These tools will help you prepare and bake the best salmon recipe with ease.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets (fresh or thawed if frozen)

  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, or thyme), chopped
